An overview of my neighborhood



I currently live in Escondido, California

"Esco" as we call it, is a reletively sleepy city in Northern San Diego.

Esco is known for agriculture, good mexican food and local wine.



Escondido was incorporated in 1888.
It is one of the oldest cities in San Diego County.

It has a population of 151,038 as of the 2020 census.
